About

Have you ever dared to imagine what it would be like to travel through the human body? Or what about visiting outer space and truly learning what it is to be part of the universe. If you have, I'm sure you're familiar with one of the best cartoon characters, Miss Frizzle. Ms. Valerie Frizzle, the magic school teacher, was the closest approximation to an expert female host. She was voiced by Lily Tomlin in the series. From 1994 to 2000, several Magic School Bus video games were released.
